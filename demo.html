What you're looking at

Saaro reframes podcasts around chapters instead of episodes. The home screen surfaces Continue Listening (your active queue, with chapter-level state) and Latest Episodes (your curated feed that bumps anything you've recently engaged with). Tap any episode to plan your listen — skip what you don't want, queue what you do — then play.

Home screen: your active queue and curated feed, side by side.

Episode page: swipe through chapter cards before you commit to listening.

Planning view: read the summary, check the tags, decide what to play.

While playing: the next chapter is always one glance away.
