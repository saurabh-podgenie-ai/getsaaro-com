Common questions

How do I get an iOS invite?

Sign up at getsaaro.com. We're onboarding testers in waves as we scale TestFlight capacity.

When will Android be available?

We're gauging interest. If you'd like to be notified — and to help us decide whether to build it — sign up on the home page.

How do I add a private podcast feed?

In the Library tab, tap the + icon and paste the RSS URL. Works with Substack, Patreon, Stratechery, and any other private feed that provides a tokenized RSS URL.

What does Saaro do with the audio of my podcasts?

Saaro sends episode audio to Google's Gemini AI to generate chapter summaries. Audio is processed once per episode and the resulting chapters are cached so subsequent users of the same episode don't re-process. See our Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my account?

Email hello@getsaaro.com with the subject "Delete my account" and we'll process it within 30 days, removing all associated data.

I'm a podcast creator and don't want my podcast processed by Saaro. How do I opt out?

See our creators page for details.
