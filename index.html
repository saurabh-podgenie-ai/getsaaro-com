Chapter-level intelligence

Every episode comes with AI-generated summaries and topic tags for each chapter. You see what's in an episode before you spend an hour finding out.

Your podcast, your edits

Skip the chapters you don't want. Restore them later if you change your mind. Queue exactly what you want to listen to next. Your editorial decisions follow you across devices.

Built for serious podcast consumers

Saaro works with any podcast — public feeds, paid Substack subscriptions, Patreon-locked shows. If it has an RSS URL, Saaro can play it.
